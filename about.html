The Generics

ABOUT

We are more than just a clothing brand. We aim to motivate and inspire. We focus on quality, originality and simplicity. Generics is an up and coming luxury fashion brand specialising in contemporary ready-to-wear women's clothing and shoes. The designer brand is for the refined, chic woman who effortlessly blends bold colors with feminine silhouettes for a modern cutting-edge look.

Intricately designed prints fused with primatic tints of hues and sheeny embellishments come together to craft a utopian design for the fashion-forward tastemaker. Our motive is to make every woman feel like a diva in our collections ranging from different sizes and styles to empower and encourage body positivity.

We aim to provide well made, amazing fitting garments that will last years and years and ideally get better with age when properly cared for. Choosing quality over fast fashion.

Our brand is committed to sustainability. This is fashion that lasts and leaves a mark!
